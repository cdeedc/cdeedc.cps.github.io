## cdeedc.cps.github.io
This website includes solutions (both explanations and code) of competitive programming problems (`cps` stands for Competitive Programming Solutions).

*Author's note*. I strongly believe that competitive programming problems need to be described first in a mathematical manner and only afterwards to be implemented. The algorithm that *lives* behind the solution is of paramount importance and requires a deep understanding and validation that it actually works and it is efficient enough to pass the given set of tests. I mainly created this blog to store some of my solutions (code snippets and brief descriptions of what the idea behind the problem is) and also since I can quickly search problems based on certain keywords. Moreover, I wanted to have a way of documenting my code, due to the fact that whenever I looked at a problem again (which I have solved a long time ago), I could not understand the code, nor could I describe the algorithm used. Addressing future readers of this website, I hope that my explanations - which will be available as soon as possible in English - will help you to understand more problems or simply to (re)discover the joy of seeing an innovative idea that leads to an elegant algorithm.
